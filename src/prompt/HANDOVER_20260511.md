### Audio Post-Mux and FPS Investigation

- User reported:
  - FPS is too low and asked whether fish-eye/alpha conversion can be optimized;
  - audio is out of sync and suggested copying original audio after all video processing is complete.
- Findings from the full `test_4k` run:
  - fish-eye/alpha pack is already GPU-side, not ffmpeg-side;
  - ffmpeg is only used to mux encoded video, and now also for post video+audio mux;
  - bottleneck is RVM inference:
    - full ResNet run: `rvm_ort_avg=36.380 ms`, throughput `26.45 fps`;

  - temporary video-only file is deleted after successful post-mux.
